GENERAL GUIDELINES

The baseline habits for high-efficiency fat burning and cellular recovery.

01. SALT INTAKE / كمية الملح

خمسة جرام يومياً (معلقة صغيرة). متمنعوش تماماً عشان تحافظ على توازن الأملاح والأداء في الجيم.

Maintain exactly 5 grams of sodium intake daily (about one teaspoon). Complete restriction is a critical mistake: sodium is vital for hydration retention, preventing muscle cramps, and generating elite cellular pumps and contraction force in the gym.

🧂
5g Target Locked

02. DIET SODA / ZERO DRINKS

مسموح (واحدة أو اتنين بالكتير). بتسد الجوع ومفيهاش سعرات، بس المية هي الأساس.

Artificially sweetened beverages are fully permitted (maximum 1 to 2 cans daily). They are incredibly effective at curbing psychological sugar cravings and filling stomach volume with zero caloric footprint. However, clean mineral water remains your primary metabolic catalyst.

🥤
Max 2 Cans Limit

03. WATER INTAKE / استهلاك المياه

من 3 لـ 4 لتر يومياً. ده الحد الأدنى عشان تضمن إن الحرق والسيستم شغالين صح.

Consume between 3 to 4 liters of clean water daily. This is the absolute physical baseline needed to run metabolic thermogenesis efficiently, optimize liver function for fat burning, and maintain muscle cellular volume.

KG
3.2 Liters/Day Target

04. SLEEP DURATION / ساعات النوم

من 7 لـ 8 ساعات يومياً. أقل من كده مفيش استشفاء ومفيش نتائج.

Secure 7 to 8 hours of high-quality, deep sleep nightly. Restricting sleep acts as a biological handbrake: it spikes cortisol (muscle-wasting hormone), suppresses testosterone, raises hunger signals, and completely compromises athletic recovery.

💤
7.5 Hrs Ideal Recovery

05. DAILY STEPS / الخطوات اليومية

من 8,000 لـ 10,000 خطوة. ده الكارديو اللي مبيجوعش وبيرفع معدل حرقك اليومي.

Averaging 8,000 to 10,000 steps daily. This is key: Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis (NEAT) is the most sustainable form of cardiovascular output. It burns massive fat reserves without spiking your appetite, keeping you lean and highly mobile.
